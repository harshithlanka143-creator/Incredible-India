Itineraries & Travel Tips

Your guide to a seamless Indian adventure.

The best time to visit most of India is during the cool, dry season, between October and March. The Himalayas are best from March to June.

Most foreign nationals require an e-Visa. You can apply online through the official Government of India website. Always check the latest regulations before you travel.

Pack light cotton clothing, a universal power adapter, mosquito repellent, sunscreen, and comfortable walking shoes. Modest clothing is recommended for religious sites.
